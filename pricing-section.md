# Pricing Plan

## Pricing Strategy

- **Starter** at **KES 1,900/month** keeps entry friction low for very small businesses while still positioning HexaPay as a serious payroll tool.
- **Growth** at **KES 5,900/month** is the best-value tier for SMEs and sits comfortably inside the target range, making it the natural upgrade path.
- **Pro** at **KES 14,900/month** feels premium but still realistic for larger businesses that need stronger controls, deployment flexibility, and support.

## Final Pricing Content

### Simple Pricing for Teams That Want to Grow

Affordable payroll and HR tools for Kenyan businesses, from first hires to multi-team operations.

#### Starter
**KES 1,900 / month**

Best for: Small businesses with 1-10 employees that need a simple, reliable payroll setup.

Features:
- Basic employee management
- Attendance tracking
- Simple payroll generation
- Payslip generation
- Basic payroll and staff reports
- Single-machine or local desktop use

CTA: Start simple with the essentials.

#### Growth
**Most Popular**

**KES 5,900 / month**

Best for: Growing businesses with 10-50 employees that need better control, collaboration, and accountability.

Features:
- Everything in Starter
- Multi-user access
- Role-based permissions
- Leave management
- Payroll approvals workflow
- Audit logging
- Excel and PDF exports
- Backup tools
- LAN or hosted backend support

CTA: Scale payroll with the right controls in place.

#### Pro
**KES 14,900 / month**

Best for: Businesses with 50+ employees or teams with more advanced payroll, compliance, and reporting needs.

Features:
- Everything in Growth
- Advanced payroll rules
- Tax, loans, and deductions support
- Full audit trail
- Cloud deployment support
- Remote access
- Automated backups and restore tools
- Priority support
- Custom branding
- API access and integration readiness
- Advanced reports and analytics

CTA: Run payroll with enterprise-grade visibility and support.

### Annual and Enterprise Note

Annual billing discounts are available for committed teams, and larger clients can request onboarding, deployment support, and tailored setup.
